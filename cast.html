Cast

The *Kill Bill* films (Vol. 1 and Vol. 2) were written and directed by Quentin Tarantino and feature an ensemble cast. Here's a list of key actors and their roles:

Main Cast:

  • 1. **Uma Thurman** – **The Bride / Beatrix Kiddo** The protagonist, a former assassin seeking revenge on those who betrayed her.
  • 2. **Lucy Liu** – **O-Ren Ishii** The leader of the Crazy 88 and one of the Bride's targets, she is a skilled assassin with a tragic backstory.
  • 3. **Vivica A. Fox** – **Vernita Green** A former member of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad (DVAS) who is one of the Bride's targets.
  • 4. **Daryl Hannah** – **Elle Driver / California Mountain Snake** A deadly and vengeful assassin who is another member of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad.
  • 5. **David Carradine** – **Bill** The leader of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad and the main antagonist, Beatrix Kiddo's former lover.
  • 6. **Michael Madsen** – **Budd** Bill’s brother and former member of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad. He lives in seclusion after leaving the life of assassination.
  • 7. **Chia Hui Liu** – **Pai Mei** A legendary martial arts master who trains the Bride in the ways of combat, appearing in *Kill Bill: Vol. 2*.
  • 8. **Julie Dreyfus** – **Sofie Fatale** Bill's lawyer and a former associate of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad.
  • 9. **Sid Haig** – **Sven** A minor character who appears in the beginning of *Kill Bill: Vol. 2*.
  • 10. **Samuel L. Jackson** – **Rufus** A character who provides some comic relief in *Kill Bill: Vol. 2*, a piano player who is seen in a flashback.
